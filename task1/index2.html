Kalvakuntla Chandrashekar Rao (born 17 February 1954), frequently known by his initials KCR, is an Indian politician currently representing the Gajwel Assembly constituency and serving as the leader of the opposition in the Telangana Legislative Assembly.[3] He is the founder and leader of the Bharat Rashtra Samithi, a state party in India.[4][5] He was the first Chief Minister of Telangana after the formation of the state and held the position for almost 10 years.
